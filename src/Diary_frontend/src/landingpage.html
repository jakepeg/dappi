Welcome to dAppiDiary

The Ultimate Homework Companion for Primary School Students

Collaborative Environment

Stay connected with your child's progress and engage with their education. dAppiDiary allows seamless collaboration between parents, teachers, and students. Stay informed about upcoming assignments, exchange messages with teachers, and provide support to your child's learning journey.

Enhanced Parental Control

Rest assured knowing you have full visibility and control over your child's academic responsibilities. With dAppiDiary, you can monitor task completion, review deadlines, and receive notifications when new assignments are added. Stay involved and help your child develop strong study habits.

Secure and Private

Your child's data and privacy are our top priorities. dAppiDiary utilizes the ICP blockchain, ensuring that all information is securely stored and protected against unauthorized access. Your child's homework remains confidential within a safe and decentralized environment.
